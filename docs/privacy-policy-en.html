Namaz Vakti - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Introduction

Namaz Vakti application ("App") respects the privacy of its users. This privacy policy explains what data our app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Location Data

The App uses your device's location to accurately calculate prayer times. Location data:

  • Is used solely to calculate prayer times
  • Is stored locally on your device
  • Is not sent to our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties

Notification Preferences

The App stores your notification preferences for prayer time reminders locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data in accordance with its own privacy policy. For more information, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

Firebase Crashlytics

Firebase Crashlytics is used to detect and fix app errors. This service may collect crash reports and device information.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. Internet connection is only used for prayer time data and advertisement content.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
